Background

I've been working in cybersecurity for over a decade, with the last several years focused specifically on cloud security. I started my career in traditional network security, but made the shift to cloud as organizations began their digital transformation journeys.

What I've learned is that cloud security isn't just network security in the cloud – it requires a fundamentally different approach. Identity becomes the new perimeter, and understanding the shared responsibility model is critical to getting security right.

I hold several industry certifications and regularly speak at security conferences, but what I'm most proud of is helping organizations prevent real attacks and respond effectively when incidents do occur.
